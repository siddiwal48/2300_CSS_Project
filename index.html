Steamdeck

The Steam Deck is a handheld gaming device developed by Valve that allows users to play PC games on the go. It combines the power of a gaming computer with the portability of a console, giving players access to their Steam library anywhere. With built-in controls, a touchscreen, and customizable settings, the Steam Deck represents a major step forward in portable gaming technology and shows how devices are becoming more powerful and flexible for everyday use.
